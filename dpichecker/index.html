How to measure

  1. Click on the blue area
  2. Move your mouse to the right and measure the distance it moved
  3. Click again when you are done moving your desired distance
  4. Input the distance your mouse moved in real life

Ensuring accuracy

  • Mousepad is recommended for accuracy
  • Make sure enhance pointer precision and mouse acceleration is turned off
  • Make sure you move your mouse as horizontal as possible (left or right)
